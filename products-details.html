Staff Hour

Experience a complete attendance management system with Staff Hour

The modern workforce has its focus on reduced errors and elimination of manual tedious tasks. Staff Hour by Veuz Concepts is your all-in-one attendance management system that is designed with an aim to ensure digital staff operations, provide real-time visibility into workforce productivity, and automate payroll. As a modern attendance management software and workforce platform, Staff Hour is built to eliminate manual tracking, reduce errors, and give your organizational workforce operations the complete control over employee time and performance.

Key Features of Attendance Management System

Seamless Integrations

Integrate with ERP systems, biometric devices, payment gateways, and internal tools for a connected workforce ecosystem.

Multi-Location Management

Manage multiple branches, departments, and teams from a single centralized platform.

Staff Self-Service Mobile App

Employees can access attendance records, salary slips, leave requests, approvals, and notifications through the Staff Hour mobile app.

Insightful Reports & Dashboards

View attendance trends, payroll summaries, productivity metrics, absentee patterns, and top performers through intuitive dashboards.

Multi-Shift & Roster Scheduling

Create and manage fixed shifts, rotational shifts, split shifts, and night shifts effortlessly across teams and locations.

Automated Payroll Management

Automatically calculate salaries based on attendance data, overtime, deductions, paid holidays, leave policies, bonuses, and advances that are fully synced with your attendance management system.

Field & Remote Staff Tracking

With GPS timelines, route history and activity logs monitor field employees in real time with our reliable employee time tracking app.

End-to-End Workforce Automation

With one workforce platform that is centralized for attendance, Staff hour attendance software handle attendance, shifts, overtime, leave, holidays, incentives, advances, and loans.

Employee Time Tracking App

Enable accurate time tracking through the Staff Hour mobile app, allowing employees to log into work hours, view attendance history, and stay informed ideal for businesses needing a reliable employee time tracking app.

Smart Attendance Tracking

As a versatile attendance management software, Staff Hour helps with flexible methods including facial recognition, QR codes, GPS punch, selfie attendance, kiosk mode, or manual approvals, track employee attendance.
